# Privacy Policy — NoRefer 2

NoRefer 2 does not collect, transmit, or sell any data.

- The header rules you create are stored **locally** in your browser
  (`chrome.storage.local`) and never leave your device.
- The extension contacts **no servers**. It has no analytics, no telemetry,
  and no accounts.
- Headers are modified on-device by the browser's built-in
  `declarativeNetRequest` engine. The extension does not read the content of
  your requests, responses, or the pages you visit.

If you uninstall the extension, its stored rules are removed by the browser.

Questions: open an issue at https://github.com/c475/NoRefer2
